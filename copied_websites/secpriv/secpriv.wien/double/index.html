Double Degree TU Wien - TU Darmstadt on IT Security

TU Wien and TU Darmstadt have established a Dual Master Degree with a specific focus on information security. Students will visit the partner university for six months, thereby benefiting from a wide range of security-oriented courses and the possibility to establish international collaborations.

The legal information as well as a course template is available here. (TU Darmstadt students should also have a look at their own institution’s website).

The registration follows the usual modalities for registration in master programs at TU Wien. Financial support for covering the three semesters abroad is provided via the Erasmus program For more information on that, please contact the International Office of TU Wien.

The application deadline for WS19/20 is February 15, 2019. For applying, please send

  • CV
  • Transcript of records
  • Motivation letter

to Christopher Vomastek. You will be informed about the outcome of your application within a couple of weeks.

For further information, please contact the coordinator of the program: Prof. Matteo Maffei.
